Sustainable Making in HCI and Beyond:
An Overview of Related Work


The goal of this website is to provide a resource for newcomers in the field of sustainable interaction design and computational fabrication so that they can quickly access recent contributions made in the field.
Webpage maintained by Zeyu Yan, Jiasheng Li, Jasmine Lu, Tingyu Cheng, Pedro Lopes , and Huaishu Peng,
For feedback and changes, please email: zeyuy/at/umd.edu or submit an entry that you see missing using this Google form.



This webpage is created using "Personal Fabrication Research in HCI and Graphics" as a template, we appreciate HCI Lab @MIT CSAIL for opensourcing the template. This webpage serves as a related work directory - the creators of this webpage do not hold the rights for these works, please contact the authors directly.
